H2 RC Grand Prix

Built and raced a hydrogen-powered RC car. Finished 7th and won most efficient driving — 30 laps on one hydrogen cell.

▶ Watch the race pt1 ▶ Watch the race pt2

We were provided with basic materials including HydroSticks and had to build a functional vehicle under strict constraints.

I took initiative across multiple areas due to limited team leadership coordinating tasks, doing 3D and body design, and handling electronics.

One of our key innovations was a custom shock absorption system to protect the CPU during rear impacts. It also helped maintain momentum after collisions.

Through this project I developed practical skills in mechanics, leadership, task planning, and team coordination.
